Recipe for Apple-Cinnamon Porridge

A brief insight

Porridge is a classic scottish recipe consisting of very simple ingredients. It was created by the poorer class but provides rich taste. It can serve as a bassis for nuts and fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts, almonds) for topping (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins or dried cranberries for topping (optional)

How to make

  1. Prepare the ingredients

    Dice the Apple:

    Peel, core, and dice the apple into small pieces.

  2. Cook the Porridge

    Combine the ingredients:

    In a medium saucepan, combine the rolled oats, water (or milk), diced apple, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.

    Bring to a boil:

    Place the saucepan over medium-high heat and bring the mixture to a boil.

    Simmer:

    Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer.
    Stir occasionally to prevent sticking and to ensure even cooking.
    Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the oats are tender and the mixture has thickened to your desired consistency.

  3. Add Sweeteners and Flavorings

    Sweeten (Optional):

    Stir in the honey or maple syrup if using, and mix well.

    Add Vanilla (Optional):

    Stir in the vanilla extract if using.

  4. Serve

    Portion:

    Divide the porridge into serving bowls.

    Add Toppings (Optional):

    Top with chopped nuts, raisins, or dried cranberries if desired.

  5. Enjoy

    Serve Immediately

    Serve the apple-cinnamon porridge while it's warm and enjoy a comforting and nutritious breakfast!
