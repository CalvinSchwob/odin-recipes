Recipe for Pizza:

Description

The pizza is a world famous dish, consisting of a pice of loaf, smeard with tomato sauce combinated with toppings like salami, ham and for example mushrooms

Needed Ingredients:

  • Pizza dought
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Lots of cheese
  • Mushrooms
    •

    Steps of making:

  • Prepare and roll the pizzadought
  • Apply the tomatosauce on the rolled dought.
  • Apply the toppings (in my case, it is salami, ham, mushrooms and lots of cheese).
  • Sprinkle a few spices on top.
  • Preheat the oven und bake the Pizza untill the crust is nice and brown and the cheese get fluid.
