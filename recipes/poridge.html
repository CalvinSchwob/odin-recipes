Recipe for Apple-Cinnamon Porridge

A brief insight

Porridge is a classic scottish recipe consisting of very simple ingredients. It was created by the poorer class but provides rich taste. It can serve as a bassis for nuts and fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts, almonds) for topping (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins or dried cranberries for topping (optional)
