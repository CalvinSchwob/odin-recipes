Recipe for Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, savory, and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

  • Serve immediately, garnished with additional cheese and black pepper.
