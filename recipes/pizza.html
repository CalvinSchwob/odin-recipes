Recipe for Pizza

Back to Index

Description

The pizza is a world famous dish, consisting of a pice of loaf, smeard with tomato sauce combinated with toppings like salami, ham and for example mushrooms

Ingredients

  • Pizza dought
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Lots of cheese
  • Mushrooms

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare and roll the pizzadought
  2. Apply the tomatosauce on the rolled dought.
  3. Apply the toppings (in my case, it is salami, ham, mushrooms and lots of cheese).
  4. Sprinkle a few spices on top.
  5. Preheat the oven und bake the Pizza untill the crust is nice and brown and the cheese get fluid.
